Innovative Infrastructure

CHART is hosted on AWS (Amazon Web Services) cloud infrastructure and connects directly to your practice's clinic. Data is analyzed remotely through the cloud through a secure encrypted connection. CHART’s report is automatically returned to the preferred destination for reading by the cardiologist. CHART includes embedded security (ISO 27001), data sovereignty and compliance to the highest medical device standards (ISO 13485).
