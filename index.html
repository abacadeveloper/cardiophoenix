What is CHART

CHART is a novel medical device that provides the functionality of 3 heart medical devices in 1.
It a standard 12 lead ECG, with echocardiography findings, and a 4x electronic stethoscopes.
Specifically unique, is that CHART is able to identify the early onset of Heart Failure,
including type, HFpEF, HFmrEF, and HFrEF as well as an estimate of LVEF with high performance.

For use in Patient Care and Telehealth settings.
